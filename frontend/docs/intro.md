# Introduction to Physical AI & Humanoid Robotics

Welcome to the comprehensive textbook on Physical AI and Humanoid Robotics. This book covers the fundamental concepts, advanced topics, and practical implementations in the field of artificial intelligence applied to physical systems and humanoid robots.

## What You'll Learn

- Fundamentals of Physical AI
- Kinematics and Dynamics of Humanoid Systems
- Control Systems for Robotics
- Sensors and Perception
- Motion Planning and Navigation
- Human-Robot Interaction
- Ethics in Robotics
- Future Directions

## Target Audience

This textbook is designed for:
- Graduate students in robotics and AI
- Researchers in the field
- Engineers working on robotic systems
- Anyone interested in the intersection of AI and physical systems

## How to Use This Book

Each chapter builds upon the previous ones, but you can also jump to specific topics that interest you. The book includes:
- Theoretical foundations
- Practical examples
- Code implementations
- Case studies
- Exercises and projects
